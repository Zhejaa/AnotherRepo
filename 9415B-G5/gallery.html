Pakku or Idu or Spoon or Dipper or Laddle
Spear, shield, axe The long spear serves to wound and bring down the opponent in battle. The curved bottom of the shield is used to pin down the struggling victim by the neck. The heavy flat blade of the double-sided axe performs the actual decapitation, and the sharply curved opposing side is used to skin the skull.
